About Us


Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne, Apple was born with a vision to revolutionize personal technology. From its beginnings in a garage with the creation of the Apple I, the company has become synonymous with innovation, redefining industries with iconic products including the Mac, iPod, iPhone and iPad. With a focus on elegant design, simplicity and functionality, Apple has established itself as a global technology leader. Today, it continues to transform the world with products and services that combine innovation, quality and a unique user experience.
